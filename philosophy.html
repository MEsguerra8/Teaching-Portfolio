My Personal Teaching Style

My style has always leaned toward the Communicative Approach...

In my class, you'll likely find:

  • Stories, songs, and videos
  • Games and creative activities
  • Group work and presentations

Yes, we study grammar and take tests—because goals matter. But I also believe learning should be fun...

My goal has always been to help students not just pass the subject, but to become lifelong learners.

My Teaching Techniques

I create a classroom where students feel comfortable, respected, and motivated to participate.

  • Build Relationships: I connect with my students so they feel safe...
  • Use Warm Language & Teacher Talk: It lowers their affective filter...
  • Engage and Empower: Through group work, role-plays, and real-life connections...
  • Practice with Purpose: Guided practice in groups. Independent practice...
  • Assess and Celebrate Growth: Through creative presentations and tests...

I'm excited to be back in the classroom—ready to inspire, support, and learn alongside my students once again.
